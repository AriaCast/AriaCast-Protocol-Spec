# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus discussion on technical outcomes
- Assume good intent

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject content that violates this Code of Conduct.
